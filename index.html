SADE

Helen Folasade Adu, professionally known as Sade, is a Nigerian-born British singer, known as the lead vocalist of her band Sade. Her voice and songwriting gives listeners soulful, jazzy, pop, and funky music that I still listen to daily and enjoy every song like I heard it for the first time. She has an expansive musical career dating from the mid-1980s to 2011 during which, hits such as "Smooth Operator", "Stronger than Pride", and "Lovers Rock" were released. The music of talented Sade continues to capture over 13 million listeners on Spotify alone.
